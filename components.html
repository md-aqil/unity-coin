Features

Permissionless listing

A project can be listed for voting on Dpad by anyone, without any centralized filtering of teams. New and anonymous teams are welcome.

Whitelisting

Multiple staking tier profiles will provide whitelisting to dedicated investors of Dpad.

Decentralized voting

Projects will be chosen democratically by the Dpad token holder’s community DAO. Although voting is optional for projects to get listed in the fundraiser

Project Audit

For the safety of the investors on Dpad, we will provide researched audits of maximum projects, through community volunteers.

Powering the DPAD protocol

The DPAD token serves three main purposes: Staking, governance
over the protocol and gas fees.

Staking

The DPAD token holders are rewarded with game theory incentives to behave in honest ways and for supporting the ecosystem. profiles for investors with different privileges like guaranteed allocation, extra allocation, headstart time, etc.

Governance

DPAD token holders have total control over the protocol. They have the rights to vote for projects, decision making for protocol upgrades and fixes.

3. Add Binance Smart Chain to your network list by clicking on the dropdown and selecting "Custom RPC".

4. Enter the config below and save.

Step 2: Get BNB in your Metamask wallet.

Step 3: Buy $DPAD (For Metamask Mobile App)

1. Open Browser in Metamask App.

  • 2. Open dpad.finance in the browser. If you have an invite link, then paste that link for referral bonus.

  • 3. Enter the BNB amount that you want to invest in the $DPAD token. Click Buy Now.

  • 4. Confirm the transaction request.

  • 5. Voila! It’s done
